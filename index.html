Lessons Learned From Snakes

After the flood, Noah inspected the ark one last time, and he came across one last pair of snakes. "Why are you still here?" he asked in surprise. "It's safe now! Go forth and multiply!"

The snakes stared at him in confusion and said, "But... we're adders; we can't multiply!"

Those snakes might have had limited math skills, but I've learned a lot from experiences with snakes.
